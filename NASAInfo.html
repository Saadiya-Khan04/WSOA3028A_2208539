Why You Should Check It Out

NASA's Website has a variety of information and images that anyone can access. Personally, I think it's good to know what's happening
at NASA because of all of the achievements that Humans are making in space exploration.
The site also contains "Image of the Day", it's fun to watch the site get updated daily with new pictures.
The coolest thing that I came across on the site is the "Missions" tab. NASA updates the site about all of the new space missions and what they aim to achieve
(The plans are very detailed), as well as new discoveries made through these missions.
