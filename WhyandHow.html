WHY AND HOW?

My character was designed to be like me, but just maybe a bit more interesting.
It is easier to put yourself into a character and talk about your own adventures, feelings and thoughts
as it is more genuine. It feels more real, although it can be slightly exaggerated.

I am still deciding how this website is going to look, I don't have a definite idea yet.
Planning a website in advance is tricky, in my opinion, I love to experiment and go along with the ideas that I find interesting.

My vision for character development will be that the content on this blog changes over time,
the images will soon start changing to how Yami explores Johannesburg, with more personal content.
