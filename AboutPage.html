What About Space?

What About Space And Why About Space?

What Will Be Displayed?

I will be using an API that I have found on the NASA website. This specific API will track the weather in Space. For now, it will only display Solar Flares.

Why This Specific API?

It seemed like the most entertaining one to play around with in the list that we were given. It's also interesting to know what the weather could be like in Space.

What Inspired Me To Create This?

As a kid, I always loved looking at the stars and wondering what else could be lurking up there. I eventually started teaching myself about Astronomy until I got to High-School. I took Science as one of my chosen subjects, I had my heart set on becoming an Astronomer and one day, working for NASA.
Science was not for me, I struggled a lot. That's how I ended up in an Art degree, following my passion for art, games and design. So I decided to take this opportunity and develop this site the way I did, to bring some nostalgia back into my life and still develop things that I am interested in, that I would like to share with other people.
